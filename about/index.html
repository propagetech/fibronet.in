About

Fiber internet, our own network

Sherie's Fibronet is a Bangalore ISP delivering fiber to the home and office on infrastructure we build and operate — not leased copper lines.

Upload speeds matter as much as downloads — for video calls, cloud backups, and working from home. Our FTTH network is built for that: symmetrical plans up to 1 Gbps, with fiber run directly to your premises.

More than 1,000 homes are already on our network. We serve residential subscribers, small businesses, and enterprises across Bangalore with reliable, high-speed broadband.

Our mission

Provide reliable, secure, fast internet so homes and businesses in Bangalore can work, learn, and connect without compromise.

Our vision

Be the trusted local fiber provider — known for our own network, transparent plans, and responsive support.
