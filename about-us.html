ABOUT US

Home /  About us
Today, commerce, communication, education, entertainment — all are happening on the Internet. Instead of being watchers, or readers, we are now participants and contributors. We’re uploading photos, videos and big work files. Fast download speeds used to be all we needed. Now, upload speeds are just as important.

This demand for bandwidth can’t be met by service providers using traditional bandwidth delivery technologies. For communities to compete for growth, improved standard of living, educational opportunity, and economic development, they must be served with fiber optic technology. Residents and businesses need the freedom to connect and the bandwidth to do it.

Sherie’s fibronet delivers bandwidth that other’s can’t match — Internet upload speeds that are just as fast as download — up to 1Gbps! This allows residential and business subscribers to participate equally with the most advanced urban areas in the Internet world of commerce, communication, education, and entertainment. We provide Internet services to bring your whole household into the digital age.

Whether you’re a business or residential customer, ready to meet your specific Internet needs with prompt, professional and reliable service

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide highly reliable, secure and fast internet services and other related services that will assist businesses, individuals and non-profit organizations in connecting to the internet, promoting their brands and reaching out to a wide range of potential customers all over the globe via the internet.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to establish a standard and world class internet service provider (ISP) company whose services and brand will not only be accepted in the state of Karnataka, but also in rest of India.
